About IDR

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training and deployment institute. We specialize in digital, cyber, and technology risk roles within regulated, high-consequence environments.

By bridging the gap between academic insight and real-world execution, we ensure our practitioners are ready for the most complex challenges in the global tech landscape.

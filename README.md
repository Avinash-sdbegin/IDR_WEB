# Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) – Website Assignment

A responsive single-page website designed for the **Institute of Digital Risk (IDR)**, an industry-led training and deployment institute focused on digital, cyber and AI risk.

---

## 🌐 Live Demo

**Live Website:** https://avinash-sdbegin.github.io/IDR_WEB/

**GitHub Repository:** https://github.com/Avinash-sdbegin/IDR_WEB

---

## 📦 Deliverables

This assignment includes:

✅ IDR Logo (Icon-only variant)  
✅ IDR Logo (Icon + text variant)  
✅ Responsive homepage (desktop and mobile)

---

## 📄 Website Sections

**Hero Section**  
Introduces IDR's mission with headline, subheading and call-to-action buttons.

**About IDR**  
Explains the institute's role in training digital risk professionals and bridging academic research with industry practice.

**Service Pillars**  
Three core areas presented using structured cards:
- **Academy** – Training and bootcamps for professionals
- **Innovation & Incubation** – Development of IP, risk models and AI governance
- **Advisory Services** – Implementation of frameworks (NIST, ISO 27001, NIS2)

**Community**  
Highlights students, early-career professionals and practitioners in regulated industries.

**Contact Section**  
Registration form for user inquiries and interest submissions.

---

## 🛠️ Technical Implementation

Built using modern web development practices:

- Semantic **HTML5**
- **CSS3 Flexbox and Grid** for layout
- **Vanilla JavaScript** for interactivity
- Sticky navigation with smooth scrolling
- Responsive design (desktop, tablet, mobile)
- Accessible color contrast and typography
- No CSS frameworks used

---

## 🎨 Design Approach

**Minimalist technology and education-focused aesthetic**

**Color Palette:**
- **Orange (#ff6b00)** – Innovation and digital transformation  
- **Black (#000000)** – Authority and security  
- **White (#ffffff)** – Clarity and accessibility

**Typography:**
- Headings: Sora (geometric, modern)
- Body: Manrope (readable, professional)

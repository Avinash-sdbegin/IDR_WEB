The IDR logo uses a cube-inspired geometric mark to represent structure, layered controls, and resilience under pressure, all of which are central to digital and AI risk. The orange, black, and white palette was chosen to balance urgency and vigilance (orange), authority and trust (black), and clarity and transparency (white). Typography in the full logo is clean and modern to reflect an education and technology-focused institution while keeping readability strong at small sizes. The icon-only and icon-plus-text variants are designed to work consistently across favicon, mobile header, web, and presentation contexts.
